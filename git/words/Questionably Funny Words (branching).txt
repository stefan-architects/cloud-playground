## 🪦 Soul‑Crushing Corporate Words  
These are the words that make you feel like your personality is being slowly replaced by a spreadsheet.

- **Synergy** — when two boring things combine to become even more boring  
- **Bandwidth** — pretending humans are Wi‑Fi routers  
- **Leverage** — use, but with a suit on  
- **Alignment** — everyone pretending they agree  
- **Stakeholder** — someone who will definitely send feedback  
- **Optimize** — “do more with less,” but make it sound strategic  
- **Pipeline** — the imaginary place where ideas go to die  
- **Touchpoint** — a meeting disguised as a word  
- **Actionable** — something you now have to do  
- **Deliverable** — homework for adults  
- **Scalable** — “we want this to be bigger, cheaper, and your problem”  
- **Resource** — a human being, but dehumanized  
- **Roadmap** — a plan that will change 14 times  
- **KPIs** — numbers that determine your fate  
- **Compliance** — fun’s natural predator  
- **Onboarding** — corporate hazing  
- **Governance** — rules about rules  
- **Cross‑functional** — chaos, but with calendars  
- **Value‑add** — something that probably doesn’t  
- **Holistic** — everything and nothing at the same time
