### 😂 **Questionably Funny Words**
- **Skedaddle** — sounds like running away in clown shoes  
- **Kerfuffle** — dramatic word for a tiny problem  
- **Bumbershoot** — an umbrella that’s trying too hard  
- **Hootenanny** — a party where banjos appear uninvited  
- **Flibbertigibbet** — someone whose brain is basically a browser with 47 tabs open  
- **Nincompoop** — the most polite insult ever invented  
- **Gobbledygook** — corporate email language
